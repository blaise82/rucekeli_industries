Orientation

Area of business and orientation

Our production operations are based in the Republic of Rwanda and Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC). In both jurisdictions, our main stakeholders and Clients varies from private construction companies, Government Institutions, Non-Governmental Organizations and individual persons.

In Rwanda, our manufacturing operations are based at Gasharu Villave, Munanira Cell (Rwafandi), Rutare Sector, Gicumbi District, Northern Province while in Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC), our factories are based at Bukavu, South Kivu Province.

Brief history

Founded in 2010, RUCEKELI INTERMIXTE Ltd is a private company specialized in the manufacturing and supply

of different kinds of construction materials and equipment. In the past Twelve (12) years of existence, we have positioned ourselves as one of the dependable partners in the supply of construction materials and equipment.

In our scope of operations, we are capable of supplying construction materials and equipment to a range of large-scale construction projects to small scale construction sites and homes.
